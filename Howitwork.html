INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Register/Login
    • New users must register with their email/phone number.
    • Existing users can log in to track their petitions.
  2. Submit a Petition
    • Fill in all required details: Category, Description, Location, and Supporting Documents (if any).
    • Provide clear and concise information about the issue.
  3. Check Petition Status
    • Track the petition progress in "My Petitions".
    • Receive SMS/Email updates about status changes.
  4. Follow Response Timelines
    • Departments will process petitions within the given timeframe.
    • Users should check notifications for updates.
  5. Respectful Communication
    • Avoid abusive or misleading language in petitions.
    • Petitions violating guidelines will be rejected.
  6. Feedback & Resolution Confirmation
    • Confirm if the grievance is resolved or request further action.
    • Provide feedback on the resolution process.
