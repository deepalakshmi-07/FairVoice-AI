About Us

Welcome to our AI-powered grievance redressal system, designed to make petition filing simple, transparent, and effective. Our platform enables citizens to submit grievances effortlessly, ensuring that every issue is properly documented and acknowledged within three days.

Once a petition is filed, our system automatically processes, categorizes, and prioritizes grievances, allowing relevant authorities to take swift action. Through real-time tracking, users can monitor updates and receive notifications at every stage, ensuring complete transparency.

If a resolution is not satisfactory, users have the option to appeal for a review, further ensuring accountability and fair outcomes. We strive to create a system where grievances are not just heard but effectively resolved within 30 days.
