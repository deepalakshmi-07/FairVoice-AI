Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

It’s an online platform designed to allow users to file petitions or grievances, track their progress in real time, and receive updates about resolutions. The system analyzes submissions and routes them to the appropriate department for prompt action.

You can register using your email address or phone number. Once registered, log in using your credentials. Registration helps keep your petition details secure and allows you to track the progress of your case.

After logging in, navigate to the “Submit Petition” section. Fill out the required fields—such as category, description, location, and any supporting documents—and submit your form. The system will generate a unique case ID for tracking purposes.

Once submitted, your petition is automatically routed to the relevant department based on its category. The system uses built-in criteria to prioritize cases, ensuring urgent matters are flagged and addressed promptly.
